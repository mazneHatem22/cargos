Accountant

Freight Accountant is a professional who specializes in managing the financial aspects of freight and transportation operations. Their role primarily involves handling the accounting and financial tasks related to freight, such as recording and analyzing transportation expenses, managing freight invoices, and ensuring accurate and timely payment to carriers and from freight brokers or shippers.

Requirements:

  • - Accounting, billing, collecting experience.
  • - Positive and determined mentality
  • - Must be able to work from home 6AM-5PM U.S central time
  • - Must have access to an updated computer and fast internet.
