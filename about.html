About Us

CARGO TRAVELS LLC: Empowering Hotshot Owners in the Transportation Industry

Founded in 2018, CARGO TRAVELS LLC has rapidly grown in the hotshot industry, specializing in shipping goods from 34ft to 40ft, weighing 10,000 to 20,000 pounds. Initially in debris transport, they transitioned to equipment hauling, assisting 200+ hotshot entrepreneurs with a single-ton truck start. Their innovative lease-on program empowers individuals with reliable equipment and work ethic, offering a chance for career change and self-managed success. With a driver-centric approach, North American-based CARGO TRAVELS empathizes with hotshot challenges, evolving through owner-operator input. Proud of its Prosper, Texas roots, the diverse team shares a commitment to quality, driving passion, and mutual success, embodying hotshot empowerment.

Why Choose Cargo Travels?

Cargo Travels LLC, a dominant industry player, stands out with its robust infrastructure. Our in-house broker team secures direct loads, ensuring a consistent flow of opportunities. Our 24/7 dispatch aids smooth operations, while the Compliance and Quality Assurance team upholds top-tier service. Prompt payment within 24 hours post-drop-off by our finance team sets us apart, allowing you to focus solely on driving. Choose Cargo Travels for a seamless and worry-free experience.

Cargo Travels: Honesty in Collaboration

At Cargo Travels, honesty is our cornerstone. We prioritize transparent communication among all stakeholders - drivers, brokers, shippers, and our team - fostering seamless collaboration and unity.

Our Focus and Commitment

Cargo Travels specializes exclusively in hotshot flatbeds. Our expertise encompasses diverse items suitable for flatbed trailers - machinery, car parts, lumber, steel, and more. We handle all things industrial. Our utmost commitment is to ensure responsible scheduling, avoiding empty trips and adverse weather conditions, driving our daily dedication.

Empowerment and Transparency

With Cargo Travels, you're in control. You can accept or decline loads as you see fit. Transparency rules the process, including payment details. As agreed, you'll receive your designated share. Our partnership is built on mutual understanding and effective communication.
